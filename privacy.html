ShareCount Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you join our waiting list and participate in our referral program.

1. Introduction 2. Definitions 3. Data Collection 4. Data Use 5. Legal Basis 6. Data Sharing 7. Referral Program 8. Data Security 9. Data Retention 10. Your Rights 11. International Transfers 12. Children's Data 13. Policy Changes 14. Contact Us

1. Introduction

ShareCount ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you join our digital wallet waiting list and participate in our referral program.

Scope: This policy applies to all personal data collected through our website, mobile applications, and other interactions related to our waiting list and referral program.

By joining our waiting list or participating in our referral program, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

2. Definitions

Key Terms

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable individual
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data (collection, recording, organization, etc.)
  • Data Subject: The individual to whom personal data relates
  • Controller: The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data
  • Processor: The entity that processes personal data on behalf of the controller
  • Referral Program: Our promotional campaign where users can earn rewards by inviting others to join our waiting list

3. Data We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our waiting list and referral program:

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you sign up for our waiting list or participate in our referral program, we may collect:

Data Category Examples Purpose
Contact Information Full name, email address, phone number Waitlist management, communication
Demographic Information Age, gender, location (if provided) Service personalization
Referral Information Referral code, referred contacts Program administration
Payment Information Bank details (for prize winners) Reward fulfillment

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain information when you interact with our services:

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referral sources
  • Cookies and Tracking: See our Cookie Policy for details

3.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Social media platforms when you interact with our content
  • Analytics providers like Google Analytics
  • Referral partners who direct you to our services

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

4.1 Waiting List Management

  • To maintain and manage our waiting list
  • To notify you when our services launch
  • To provide updates about our product development

4.2 Referral Program Operation

  • To track and verify referrals
  • To calculate and award referral bonuses
  • To display referral leaderboards
  • To prevent fraud and abuse of the program

4.3 Service Improvement

  • To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • To develop new features and functionality
  • To conduct market research and analysis

4.4 Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To respond to legal requests and prevent harm
  • To enforce our terms and policies

5. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data under the following legal bases:

Processing Activity Legal Basis
Waitlist management Contractual necessity
Referral program operation Legitimate interest
Marketing communications Consent (where required)
Legal compliance Legal obligation

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your personal data with the following categories of recipients:

6.1 Service Providers

We engage third-party companies to perform services on our behalf:

  • Email Service Providers: For sending waitlist updates and referral notifications
  • Analytics Providers: For analyzing user behavior and program effectiveness
  • Payment Processors: For distributing referral rewards to winners
  • Cloud Hosting Providers: For secure data storage and processing

6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6.3 Business Transfers

In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, or acquisition, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

6.4 Referral Program Specifics

When you refer someone to our waiting list:

  • We will inform the referred individual that you referred them
  • Your first name and referral count may be displayed on public leaderboards
  • We will not disclose your contact information to referred individuals without your consent

7. Referral Program Details

Our referral program has specific privacy considerations:

7.1 Program Mechanics

  • Each participant receives a unique referral code
  • We track when this code is used to join our waiting list
  • Referral counts are displayed on a public leaderboard
  • Top referrers may receive prizes as described in our program terms

7.2 Information Collected for Referrals

For each referral, we collect:

  • The referring user's ID and code
  • The referred user's contact information
  • Timestamp of the referral
  • IP address and device information for fraud prevention

7.3 Prize Fulfillment

If you win a referral prize:

  • We may require additional verification information
  • This may include government-issued ID and tax information
  • Payment details will be collected securely for prize distribution
Note: Referral program terms and prize eligibility are governed by our Terms of Service.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

8.1 Technical Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms

8.2 Organizational Measures

  • Privacy and security training for staff
  • Data protection impact assessments
  • Incident response procedures
  • Vendor security assessments

8.3 Security Limitations

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your information.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

Data Type Retention Period Rationale
Waitlist information Until service launch + 1 year Service onboarding
Referral program data 3 years after program ends Program administration, tax compliance
Prize winner information 7 years after payment Financial record keeping
Website usage data 26 months Analytics purposes

After these periods, we will either delete or anonymize your data so it can no longer be associated with you.

10. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

10.1 Access and Portability

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Receive a copy of your data in a structured format

10.2 Correction and Deletion

  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data

10.3 Processing Restrictions

  • Object to certain processing activities
  • Request restriction of processing in specific circumstances

10.4 Withdraw Consent

Where we rely on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing.

10.5 Complaint

You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

How to Exercise Rights: Submit requests to privacy@sharecount.co.za with "Privacy Request" in the subject line. We may require identity verification before processing requests.

11. International Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own:

11.1 Transfer Mechanisms

We implement appropriate safeguards for international transfers, including:

  • EU Standard Contractual Clauses
  • Adequacy decisions where applicable
  • Other legally-approved transfer mechanisms

11.2 Locations

Our service providers may be located in:

  • United States
  • European Economic Area
  • Other jurisdictions as needed for service delivery

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 18:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children
  • If we discover we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly
  • Parents or guardians may contact us to request deletion of their child's information

  • Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance
